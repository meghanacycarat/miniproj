Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register a criminal?

To register a criminal, go to the "Manage Criminals" page and click on the "Register Criminal" button. Fill in the required details and submit the form.

How do I manage cases?

You can manage cases by navigating to the "Manage Cases" page. Here, you can add new cases, update existing ones, and assign officers to cases.

How do I generate reports?

To generate reports, visit the respective page of what report you want. After that navigate to the respective table where the report is displayed, click on "View File", and then download it using the provided download button.
